PBJ Sandwich

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich diagonally cut in half.

A simple and delicious recipe. This sandwich only calls for three ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the other piece of bread
  4. Put the two halves together
